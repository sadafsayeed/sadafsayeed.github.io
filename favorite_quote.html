Quote of the Day
The cold water does not get warmer if you jump late
This is a truth that shook me. It reminded me that waiting only makes fear grow stronger. When I first started running, I was unfit, struggling to breathe after half a mile. But I jumped in anyway. I kept showing up, pushing through the pain, until I crossed my first 5K finish line. That moment proved to me that grit matters more than talent. You do not have to feel ready, you just have to start.
